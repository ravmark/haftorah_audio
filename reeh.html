On this page you will find a recording of the first few verses of your haftorah (to help you get started), as well as the blessings before and after the Haftorah.

** While you may be tempted to try and memorize the melody of your Haftorah WITHOUT learning the trope, it is MUCH easier in the long run to learn the trope symbols first, and only then apply them to your Haftorah.

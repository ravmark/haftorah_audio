<font color="blue" size="+0"><b><u>Audio Download Instructions</u></b></font>
<br>
<font color="blue" size="-1"><b> (including how to install files onto your iPod):</b></font><br><br>

<b>Step 1</b><br>

Download the .mp3 audio files from this page - by clicking on above links. Select "save" to choose a location on your computer to 

save the audio file.<br><br>

<u><b>Hint</b></u>: Remember the download location on your hard drive.  (When in doubt, simply download the files to your 

"desktop".)
<br>
Downloaded files can now be played directly on your computer, copied onto a CD, or mp3 player.

<br><br>

[Alternatively, you can also save the audio files on your hard drive by doing a "right mouse click" on the filename (Windows-based 

computers) and click "save target as" or "save link as".  Then choose where on your computer you want to save the file. Mac OS / 

Apple users do a "command click" to save the file on their computer.]
<br><br>


<font color="blue" size="-1">
-- Continue with the following steps to install the audio files onto an iPod player:</font>
<br><br>

<b>Step 2</b><br>

Open your iTunes software.
<br><br>

<b>Step 3</b><br>

Click on "File," and then "Add to Library."
<br><br>

<b>Step 4</b><br>

Locate audio files on your hard drive. <br>Select a file and click "Open" to add it to your library.
<br><br>

<b>Step 5</b><br>

To update your iPod with the new files, click the "sync" button. <br><br>

The Haftorah should now be on your iPod and ready to play. The files will either show up in an album called "Haftorah Audio" or you 

can find it under artist: "Rabbi Mark Zimmerman"
